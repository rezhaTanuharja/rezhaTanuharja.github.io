This project explores various factors that may affect the life expectancy in a country. It reveals two interesting observations:

  • GDP per capita only correlates positively to life expectancy and negatively to mortality rate in wealthy countries.
  • Countries with low GDP per capita are prone to economic inequality, leading to groups of people living in extreme poverty, leading to high mortality rate.
